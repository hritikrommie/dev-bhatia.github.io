Data Visualization for
Mouse Behavior Research

The Shtrahman Lab at UC San Diego
View on GitHub
Built automated data visualization daily email system with Python. By computing datapoints from the experiement's extenise SQL database, researchers can see experiement progression and results to better study cognitive pattern recognition in mice with different chemical solutions. Through this, I learned how to analyze large datasets (250,000+ unique entries) with computationally efficient code - decreasing runtime by 10x (from 20 minutes to less than 2). Remote & Shared 3D Printing

View presentation
For my Product Engineering class my team and created a full stack web application to connect people who want to 3D print with those who own printers. Users can upload files, share them remotely with partners registered on our site, who can then download the part for them. We worked using Agile methos (Gantt Chart, SCRUM, etc) and went from an idea to deploying our solution in just 8 weeks.
