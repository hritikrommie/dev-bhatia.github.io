Resume Evolution

During school, I work on-campus at the UCSD Career Center mainly helping peers with resume critiques, job/career search, and occasionally presentations. By this point I've critiqued 300+ resumes. Some people have come by multiple times and it's great to see their resumes get better. That made me wonder - how have my resumes changed since I started college? I've saved almost every resume I've made (~50 different ones by now). So with some screenshots, this site to create a GIF, and Canva... here it is!
