Resume Evolution

During school, I work on-campus at the UCSD Career Center mainly helping peers with resume critiques, job/career search, and occasionally presentations. By this point I've critiqued 300+ resumes. Some people stop by multiple times and it's great to see their resumes evolve to be better. That made me wonder: how have my resumes changed since I started college? I've saved almost every resume I've made on my drive (~50 different ones by now). So with all their screenshots, this site to create a GIF, and Canva... here it is!
